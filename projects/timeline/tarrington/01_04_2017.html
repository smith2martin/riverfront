Dock and Boat Storage Installed

The installation of the multi-level dock in the Park was a monumental step forward for the usability and enjoyment of the Park.

Together with multiple boat storage racks, residents are now able to enjoy a prime benefit of living directly on the river: easy access to launch their personal watercraft. Boating, fishing, and swimming have quickly become some of the most popular activities down at the Park.

Don't forget to register your boat!
